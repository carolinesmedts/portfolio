About Me

My name is Caroline Smedts. I'm a graphic and digital media student at Artesis and Plantijn Hogeschool in Antwerp.
This website gives you an overview of some of my work that I've made for school. But also my links to my social media, if you wanna see more of me.

My biggest interests are Webdesign, Social Media and Public Relations.
My biggest inspirations I get from travelling, interior design and anything tropical. I'm not afraid to use colour, but I also like a very clean design with a lot of white.
